The 2nd Climate Farming Conference

The food industry needs to undergo a fundamental shift towards regeneration. The only way we can achieve this is through systemic collaboration.The 2nd Climate Farming Conference is a 3-day gathering for the grassroots leaders of the regenerative movement to meet, exchange & shape the future of regenerative agriculture with a unified voice.
